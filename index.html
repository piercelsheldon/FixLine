FAQ

Common questions

Is the Q&A really free?
Yes, completely. Submit your question and I'll call you back and walk you through the fix at no charge. You only pay if you want parts sourced or extended support time.
Do I need to bring my computer anywhere?
Nope — everything is done over the phone. I'll guide you step by step through fixing the issue yourself. If we need remote screen access, we can set that up too.
How does the parts ordering work?
I identify the exact part you need, find the best price from a reputable supplier, and place the order for you. You pay the part cost plus a $15 service fee. I'll track the shipment and help you install it when it arrives.
What if you can't fix my problem?
If I can't help, I'll tell you honestly — and I'll point you in the right direction. You'll never be charged for a problem I couldn't solve.
What areas do you serve?
Since everything is done by phone, I can help anyone in the US. Parts can be shipped anywhere with a US address.
